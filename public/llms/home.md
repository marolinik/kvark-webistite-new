# KVARK Homepage

Canonical URL: https://kvark.ai/

## Purpose
The homepage introduces KVARK as a European sovereign agentic AI platform for regulated industries, deployed on-premise or fully air-gapped inside customer-controlled infrastructure.

## Main messages
- Enterprise AI that never leaves your walls: all data and AI processing stay inside the organization's infrastructure.
- Sovereign by deployment, governed by design, agentic in operation.
- Compliance mapping to the EU AI Act, GDPR, NIS2, DORA and CRA: permission-aware retrieval, data lineage, audit logging, guardrails, model lifecycle management, SBOM.
- Permission-aware AI mirrors source-system access controls per user and role (fail-closed), with SSO (Microsoft Entra, Okta), RBAC and AD/LDAP.
- Target industries: public sector and national security, financial services, healthcare and life sciences, critical infrastructure.
- Proof: in production at AOFI (Export Credit Agency of the Republic of Serbia) — 80% less time finding information, 50–70% faster AML research, ESG reporting cycle reduced from six months to two weeks.
- 86% lower five-year total cost of ownership versus equivalent cloud consumption; optional liquid-cooled 4U8G GPU server; hardware-agnostic.
- Deployment options: air-gapped, on-premise (Docker Compose or Kubernetes/Helm), private cloud and hybrid; fully documented open-source substrate (PostgreSQL, NATS, MinIO, MLflow, OnlyOffice, local open-weight LLMs).
- Built and operated in Europe by Egzakta Group (founded 2006, 240+ employees, 350+ enterprise projects).
- Visitors are guided toward demo/contact actions for commercial onboarding.
