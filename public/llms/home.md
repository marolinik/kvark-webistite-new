# KVARK Homepage

Canonical URL: https://kvark.ai/

## Purpose
The homepage introduces KVARK as a sovereign enterprise AI platform focused on secure deployment inside customer-controlled infrastructure.

- Enterprise AI can run without cloud dependency.
- Data sovereignty and privacy are central product requirements.
- KVARK positions itself for regulated and security-sensitive environments.
- Visitors are guided toward demo/contact actions for commercial onboarding.
