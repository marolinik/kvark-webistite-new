# KVARK Sovereign AI Insights

Canonical URL: https://kvark.ai/resources/insights

## Purpose
The Insights hub is KVARK's sovereign-AI knowledge base: evidence-first analysis for CISOs, DPOs and CIOs at regulated European enterprises. Every article is grounded in cited primary sources (regulatory texts, analyst data, model documentation).

## Categories
- Briefing — dated monthly digests of sovereign-AI developments (regulation milestones, sovereign-cloud market data, enforcement actions, open-weight model releases, EU compute buildout).
- Regulation — EU AI Act timeline analysis (post-Digital-Omnibus), NIS2 and DORA obligations for AI platforms, CRA/SBOM requirements.
- Open Models — the open-weight model landscape for enterprise self-hosting (Mistral, DeepSeek, Qwen, Gemma, European models such as Apertus and OpenEuroLLM), licensing distinctions, selection guidance.
- Infrastructure — air-gapped AI architecture, on-premise TCO economics versus per-token cloud pricing, GPU utilization.
- Guides — practitioner checklists: the ten-question sovereignty test, permission-aware retrieval, inference-time residency versus storage residency.
